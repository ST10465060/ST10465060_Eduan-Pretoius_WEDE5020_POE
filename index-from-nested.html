About the project

Healing Hooves is a South African equine‑assisted therapy initiative where people and rescued horses heal together. With 14+ years of horsemanship and equine‑assisted experience guiding the work, we offer individual and family sessions, learning programs for youth and schools, group workshops, and corporate team building. Our approach builds emotional regulation, confidence, communication and connection.

Trust signals: ethical horse care, trauma‑informed practice, and a qualified facilitator. No prior horse experience required.

What we offer

All sessions are ground‑based unless otherwise specified.

Equine‑Assisted Psychotherapy

Support with stress, anxiety, trauma recovery or life transitions.

Equine‑Assisted Learning

Skills for communication, confidence, empathy and problem‑solving.

Family Sessions

Strengthen relationships and practice calmer conflict resolution.

Workshops & Groups

Topic‑based programs that offer tools and peer support.

Corporate Team Building

Experiential days that develop trust, collaboration and feedback.
