Our services

All sessions are ground‑based unless otherwise specified. No prior horse experience required.

Equine‑Assisted Psychotherapy

Support with stress, anxiety, trauma recovery, or life transitions through experiential work with horses.

Format: 50–60 minutes, 1:1

Outcomes: improved emotional awareness, resilience and coping.

Equine‑Assisted Learning

Skills‑focused sessions for youth, schools and community groups: communication, confidence, leadership and problem‑solving.

Format: 60–90 minutes, individual or small groups

Outcomes: measurable skill development and classroom transfer.

Family Sessions

Strengthen relationships and practice calmer conflict resolution with guided activities.

Format: 60–90 minutes, families/caregivers and children

Outcomes: clearer roles, calmer conflict resolution and shared connection.

Workshops & Groups

Topic‑based programs (emotional regulation, educator wellbeing, grief support).

Format: half‑day, full‑day, or multi‑week

Outcomes: psychoeducation, peer support, practical tools.

Corporate Team Building

Experiential days that develop trust, collaboration and feedback.

Format: half‑day or full‑day

Outcomes: stronger teams and durable skills.

Notes: Safety briefing and risk assessment precede activities. Availability by booking; pricing on request.
