About Healing Hooves

Bridging animal rescue and mental wellbeing in South Africa.

Our story

Founded in 2025, Healing Hooves was created to bring rescued horses and people together in safe, trauma‑informed spaces. With 14+ years of horsemanship and equine‑assisted facilitation guiding the work, we support individuals, families, schools and teams through experiential sessions that build regulation, confidence, communication and connection.

Mission, vision and values

Mission

To facilitate personal growth and healing together with rescued horses.

Vision

Accessible, compassionate equine‑assisted support that helps people thrive.

Values

Compassion • Safety • Dignity • Evidence‑informed practice • Community

Our approach

  • Ground‑based sessions (non‑riding) unless otherwise specified.
  • Trauma‑informed, consent‑based interactions with clear boundaries.
  • Safety briefing and risk assessment precede all activities.
  • No prior horse experience required.
